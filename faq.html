About Glowing Oceans

Glowing Oceans is an immersive indoor experience where visitors can walk through glowing lagoons, explore interactive touch pools, and discover the beauty of bioluminescent plankton. With sensory tiles that light up as you move and educational activities that explore the science behind bioluminescence, it’s a magical journey that connects you with the wonders of the ocean’s ecosystems.

Glowing Oceans is the first of its kind, focusing entirely on the tiny, glowing superstars of the ocean—bioluminescent plankton! Traditional aquariums tend to roll out the red carpet for the big guys like sharks and whales, but they often miss out on showcasing the magic of bioluminescence. We spotted this gap (as rare in aquariums as seeing bioluminescence in nature!) and used our expertise in growing glowing plankton to create an immersive experience where you can literally step into the light. Hey, Traditional Aquariums: Want in on the glow? Call us about our traveling exhibit!

Plankton play a vital role in marine ecosystems. These tiny organisms produce more than 50% of the oxygen we breathe and help sequester large amounts of CO2, making them essential to life on Earth. At Glowing Oceans, you’ll learn about their incredible contributions to our planet's health.

The plankton in Glowing Oceans are completely non-toxic and safe to interact with. While some types of dinoflagellates are responsible for harmful algal blooms, commonly known as red tides, the species we showcase here are harmless. The exhibit has been thoughtfully designed to ensure the safety of both visitors and the plankton. We invite you to step in and enjoy the glow!

Glowing Oceans promotes ocean conservation through sustainable practices like growing plankton in closed-loop aquaculture systems. A portion of the proceeds from the entry fees supports marine conservation initiatives aimed at protecting fragile ecosystems and advancing ocean health.

No, the plankton used in Glowing Oceans are not harmed by the experience. The exhibit is carefully designed to maintain the health and sustainability of the bioluminescent organisms while allowing visitors to interact with this extraordinary natural phenomenon.
What To Expect

Yes, Glowing Oceans is meant to be experienced barefoot. Shoes are not allowed in the exhibit, and lockers are provided for storage.

Yes, the exhibit features shallow seawater. No swimming, sitting, or laying in the water is allowed.

The experience typically lasts between 45 and 60 minutes, depending on how much time you spend interacting with the exhibits.

Yes, photography is allowed for personal use. However, flash photography, professional equipment, and tripods are not permitted. Share your experience using #GlowingOceans and tag us @GlowingOceans_Official.

Weapons, sharp objects, outside food or drinks, alcohol, light-up accessories that could distract from the experience, and anything that may disturb other visitors are prohibited. Bags may be subject to search upon entry.

No, smoking and vaping are not allowed on the premises.

Suitability & Accessibility

Yes, Glowing Oceans is designed for visitors of all ages. Children under 3 enter free, and there are sensory experiences for all. Children under 13 must be accompanied by an adult.

Yes, the exhibit is wheelchair accessible. Detour routes are available for visitors who prefer to avoid certain features. Please ask staff for assistance.
Health & Special Considerations

The experience takes place in a very dark environment to allow the bioluminescence to shine. If you have sensitivities to dark or confined spaces, you should use caution. Please consult our staff if you have any health concerns before entering the exhibit.

Young children must be supervised at all times, especially in the water areas. Bringing a change of clothes is recommended in case they get wet.

Please contact us in advance for any specific accessibility requirements, and we’ll do our best to assist you.
