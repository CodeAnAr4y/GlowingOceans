Step into a World of Living Light

Experience the magic of bioluminescence in an immersive journey that expands your mind, ignites your senses, and sparks possibilities for a brighter world.

Ignite Imagination
Bioluminescence in Glowing Oceans is produced by dinoflagellates, a type of marine plankton that light up in response to mechanical stimulation.
Immerse Yourself
Visitors delve into a world where enchanting lagoons shimmer with bioluminescence, revealing the hidden wonders of our planet’s luminescent marvels.

Tiny Science.
Epic Experience!

At Glowing Oceans, the main attraction is a
naerly microscopic plankton that plays a vital
role in sustaining life on Earth - producing over
half the oxygen we breathe and sequestering
vast amounts of CO2.
