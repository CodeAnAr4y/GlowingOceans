Immerse yourself in the wonders of bioluminescence with the Mushlume UFO®. This uniquely designed aquarium is a solar-powered marvel, transforming your living space with the magic of the ocean.
  • Interactive Galaxy of Life: Within this celestial showpiece lies a living, interactive galaxy of bioluminescent algae. These microorganisms emit a cosmic blue glow when swirled at night.
  • Nature's Light Show in Your Hands: Powered by dinoflagellates that thrive on natural sunlight, water, and simple nutrients, the Mushlume UFO® lets you hold one of nature's most magical phenomena in your hands.
  • Educational and Mesmerizing: More than a visual spectacle, the Mushlume UFO® offers an intriguing lesson in marine biology and the science of bioluminescence, making it ideal for educational purposes.
  • Sustainably Illuminated: Embracing sustainable design, the aquarium minimizes environmental impact while maximizing visual enchantment.
Enter a world of living light. Let the Mushlume UFO® transform your space into a cosmic bioluminescent ocean, and witness the enchanting dance of light and life, night after night.
Experience Bioluminescence: Dive into the magical world of bioluminescence right at home with the Lumie.
Solar-Powered Microorganisms: Just like many houseplants, dinoflagellates use low light during the day to produce their own energy.
Nighttime Glow: As night falls, these tiny plankton become the life of the party, glowing brilliantly with a gentle swirl.
Ocean's Natural Light Show: In the wild, bioluminescent plankton create stunning blue waves of light in the ocean. The Lumie brings this mesmerizing display to your space.
Eco-Friendly and Educational: Dinoflagellates play a crucial role in our ecosystem by using CO2 and producing oxygen. In fact, more than half of the air we breathe comes from plankton, making the Lumie not just a spectacle, but also an educational tool.
