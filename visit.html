What to Expect
As you step through glowing lagoons, each movement ignites a cascade of shimmering bioluminescence, transforming the waters beneath you into a living light show. Explore touch pools where a simple swirl of your hand brings glowing organisms to life, or glide over sensory tiles that spark waves of light with every step. Each part of the journey draws you deeper into the enchanting world of the ocean’s most mesmerizing phenomenon. At Glowing Oceans, you can expect a magical and unforgettable adventure designed to ignite your senses, inspire wonder, and connect you with the beauty and mystery of the sea.

What To Wear
Dress Comfortably
The exhibit includes shallow water areas and touch pools. We recommend wearing clothes you can easily adjust, like rolling up your pants and sleeves. Shorts are highly recommended.
